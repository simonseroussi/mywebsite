Who am I?

I was born and raised near Paris, in a French town called Meudon. Don't worry, most Parisians have never heard of it. At 18, I moved from the 45,000 people municipality to the 20,000,000+ inhabited Shanghai and Beijing. I think European Megalopoles are cool too, so I also went to Madrid for a while. Today, NYC undeniably has my heart.

I am passionate about technology. I have a soft spot for products in education and talent acquisition. Some day, I'd like to launch a platform helping people find their career paths with career-shadowing and mentorship, pursuing my project Daytern.

I love to bike, especially in NY's urban jungle (it's me in the video up there ↑).

Litterature is my escape. I'm currently into Robert Musil's The Confusions of Young Törless and you'll always find Benjamin Graham's The Intelligent Investor on my nightstand.

You guessed it, I'm also a value-investing devotee. There is something thrilling about owning shares of a company that interest you. I also code sometimes, but I prefer talking to people. AIs got nothing on us (yet).
